PUP RE-ADMISSION CENTER

Our Readmission Center is designed to assist former students like you in seamlessly returning to Polytechnic University of the Philippines. Whether you took a leave of absence for personal reasons, explored other opportunities, or faced challenges that required time away, we are committed to helping you resume your education.

  • Personalized Support Our dedicated team is here to provide individualized assistance throughout the readmission process. From understanding requirements to addressing concerns, we're with you every step of the way.
  • Flexible Options We recognize that circumstances vary, and we offer flexible readmission options to accommodate your unique situation. Whether you're seeking full-time enrollment or a part-time return, we strive to make the transition as smooth as possible.
  • Academic Excellence PUP is renowned for its commitment to academic excellence. By choosing to return, you'll have the opportunity to continue your education within a supportive and intellectually stimulating environment.
